For Teachers

Setup & Reset Tools

Starting fresh or setting up for the first time? Use these tools to configure your HallPass system at the beginning of the year.

1
QR Code Generator

Generate the 30 static QR codes (qr_01 through qr_30) for your students. Print and laminate these at the start of the year.
2
Schedule Generator

Configure your Red/Black day schedules with student rosters. Generates formatted code to copy into your config file.
3
Diagnostic Tool

Test QR codes and verify they're syncing correctly with your schedule. Shows the unique ID generated for each scan.
4
Setup Documentation

Step-by-step instructions for Firebase setup, schedule configuration, and system deployment. Everything you need to get started.

Key Features

Red/Black Day Support Same 30 QR codes work for different students on different schedule days
Regular & Late Start Flexible scheduling with adjusted period times for late start days
Auto-Reset Students automatically marked "in" at period end if they forget to scan back
Complete Logging Every scan tracked with timestamps, durations, and action types
Rich Analytics Daily, weekly, and rolling averages with per-student filtering
Cloud Synced Powered by Firebase for instant updates across all devices
