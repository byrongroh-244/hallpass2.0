Generate static QR codes for your HallPass system

How to Use

This tool generates 30 static QR codes (qr_01 through qr_30) for your classroom:

  • Step 1: Click "Generate QR Codes" to create all 30 codes
  • Step 2: Preview the codes below
  • Step 3: Click "Download All as ZIP" to save them
  • Step 4: Print and laminate the QR codes
  • Step 5: Assign them to students (the same 30 codes can be reused for different students on Red/Black days)
